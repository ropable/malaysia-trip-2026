# Malaysia Family Trip Planning - December 2026

Planning and record-keeping repository for a family trip to Malaysia from **28 November to 17 December 2026**.

## Trip Overview

- **Duration:** 19 nights / 20 days
- **Family size:** 4 people
- **Route:** Langkawi → Penang → Ipoh → Melaka → Kuala Lumpur

### Accommodation Summary

- **Langkawi:** 6 nights
- **Penang:** 3 nights
- **Ipoh:** 4 nights
- **Melaka:** 2 nights
- **Kuala Lumpur:** 4 nights

## Key Information

### Important Dates

- **Departure:** 28 November 2026 (Langkawi arrival)
- **Return:** 17 December 2026 (KL departure)
- **Vaccination appointment:** September 2026 (6–8 weeks before departure)
- **MDAC (Malaysia Digital Arrival Card):** Required within 3 days of departure

### Entry Requirements

- **Visa:** Not required for Australian passport holders (visa-free for up to 90 days)
- **Passport:** Must be valid for at least 6 months from arrival date
- **Travel insurance:** Recommended
