# Malaysia Trip Itinerary - December 2026

## Overview

**Trip duration:** 19 nights / 20 days (28 November – 17 December 2026)  
**Family size:** 4 people  
**Destinations:** Langkawi → Penang → Ipoh → Melaka → Kuala Lumpur

---

## Itinerary Summary

### Segment 1: Langkawi (6 nights)

- **Dates:** 28 November – 3 December 2026
- **Arrival:** Flight into Langkawi International Airport (28 Nov)
- **Accommodation:** 6 nights, [Wings by Croske Resort](https://www.booking.com/hotel/my/wings-by-croske.en-gb.html)
- **Departure:** 4 December

**Popular Activities:**

- **Langkawi SkyCab & Sky Bridge** — Scenic cable car ride up Mount Mat Cincang with jungle canopy views and thrilling sky bridge walk
  [![Langkawi SkyCab & Sky Bridge](../static/img/langkawi-skycab-sky-bridge.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/97653867@N07/20150189308)
- **Underwater World Langkawi** — Large aquarium with 15-meter tunnel, sharks, rays, penguins (great for kids)
  [![Underwater World Langkawi](../static/img/underwater-world-langkawi.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/17868205@N00/241278353)
- **Kilim Karst Geoforest Park** — Boat tour or kayaking through mangroves, limestone caves, and eagle habitats
  [![Kilim Karst Geoforest Park](../static/img/kilim-karst-geoforest-park.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=91731969)
- **Pantai Cenang Beach** — Popular family beach with shallow swimming and beachside restaurants
  [![Pantai Cenang Beach](../static/img/pantai-cenang-beach.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=115201524)
- **3D Art in Paradise Museum** — Interactive trick art museum, perfect for family photos
  [![3D Art in Paradise Museum](../static/img/3d-art-in-paradise-museum.png)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=173995432)
- **Island Hopping Tours** — Visit nearby islands like Pulau Dayang Bunting with swimming and snorkeling
  [![Island Hopping Tours](../static/img/island-hopping-tours.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/37796692@N05/9183977994)
- **Telaga Tujuh (Seven Wells) Waterfall** — Beautiful natural pools suitable for kids
  [![Telaga Tujuh (Seven Wells) Waterfall](../static/img/telaga-tujuh-seven-wells-waterfall.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/17099096@N00/369112699)

**Notes:** Gateway beach destination; mix of adventure and relaxation

### Segment 2: Penang (3 nights)

- **Dates:** 4 December – 6 December 2026
- **Arrival:** 4 December
- **Transport from Langkawi:** Domestic flight or ferry (~2 hours)
- **Accommodation:** 3 nights, [Hotel Panaga](https://www.booking.com/hotel/my/penaga.en-gb.html)
- **Departure:** 7 December

**Popular Activities:**

- **UNESCO World Heritage Zone (Georgetown)** — Explore colonial, Chinese, Indian, and Malay architecture in historic city center
  [![UNESCO World Heritage Zone (Georgetown)](../static/img/unesco-world-heritage-zone-georgetown.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/43661283@N00/16312155182)
- **Street Art & Ernest Zacharevic Murals** — Walking tour of Armenian Street and surrounding alleys with famous street art
  [![Street Art & Ernest Zacharevic Murals](../static/img/street-art-ernest-zacharevic-murals.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/17868205@N00/15538831438)
- **Clan Jetties** — Visit unique wooden stilt houses of Chew Jetty for cultural insights
  [![Clan Jetties](../static/img/clan-jetties.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/56001877@N04/12048420395)
- **Penang Hill & Funicular Train** — Panoramic views of Georgetown, colonial-era buildings, and Habitat nature park
  [![Penang Hill & Funicular Train](../static/img/penang-hill-funicular-train.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/77742560@N06/12270988905)
- **Kek Lok Si Temple** — Massive Buddhist temple complex with seven-tier pagoda and giant Buddha statues
  [![Kek Lok Si Temple](../static/img/kek-lok-si-temple.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/124790945@N06/33062951286)
- **Pinang Peranakan Mansion** — Heritage house museum showcasing Straits Chinese opulent lifestyle
  [![Pinang Peranakan Mansion](../static/img/pinang-peranakan-mansion.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=12764841)
- **Cheong Fatt Tze (Blue Mansion)** — Striking blue heritage mansion with guided tours
  [![Cheong Fatt Tze (Blue Mansion)](../static/img/cheong-fatt-tze-blue-mansion.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/52046304@N00/3346785404)
- **Hawker Food Tours** — Sample Penang's famous char kway teow, assam laksa, nasi kandar (Chulia Street Night Market)
  [![Hawker Food Tours](../static/img/hawker-food-tours.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/53663438@N00/2674495802)
- **Little India & Chinatown** — Cultural districts with shops, music, and authentic cuisine
  [![Little India & Chinatown](../static/img/little-india-chinatown.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/14347751@N00/3098393235)

**Notes:** Rich cultural heritage and incredible food; walkable city

### Segment 3: Ipoh (4 nights)

- **Dates:** 7 December – 10 December 2026
- **Arrival:** 7 December
- **Transport from Penang:** Train (ETS, ~1.5–2 hours) or car (~2–2.5 hours)
- **Cost reference:** RM 60–100 per person by train (~AUD 18–30)
- **Accommodation:** 4 nights, [Sojourn Spa Hotel](https://www.booking.com/hotel/my/sojourn-guest-house-ipoh.en-gb.html)
- **Departure:** 11 December

**Popular Activities:**

- **Kek Lok Tong Cave Temple** — Peaceful limestone cave with Buddhist statues, gardens, and tranquil ponds
  [![Kek Lok Tong Cave Temple](../static/img/kek-lok-tong-cave-temple.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/15912013@N02/6220018649)
- **Perak Tong Cave Temple** — Impressive giant Buddha statue, colorful murals, hilltop viewpoint
  [![Perak Tong Cave Temple](../static/img/perak-tong-cave-temple.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/9905418@N05/51797683085)
- **Sam Poh Tong Cave Temple** — Serene temple structures inside a cave with turtle ponds
  [![Sam Poh Tong Cave Temple](../static/img/sam-poh-tong-cave-temple.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/15912013@N02/6220117269)
- **Old Town Heritage Trail** — Colonial buildings, historic banks, mosques tracing tin-mining history
  [![Old Town Heritage Trail](../static/img/old-town-heritage-trail.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=152116234)
- **Concubine Lane, Market Lane & Kong Heng Square** — Colorful colonial architecture, boutique shops, street art, local cafes
  [![Concubine Lane, Market Lane & Kong Heng Square](../static/img/concubine-lane-market-lane-kong-heng-square.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/76258517@N03/20671458422)
- **Tasik Cermin (Mirror Lake)** — Hidden lake encircled by limestone cliffs, boat rides through scenic tunnel
  [![Tasik Cermin (Mirror Lake)](../static/img/tasik-cermin-mirror-lake.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/76258517@N03/25176690734)
- **Lost World of Tambun** — Theme park with water rides, natural hot springs, petting zoo, adventure activities
  [![Lost World of Tambun](../static/img/lost-world-of-tambun.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/17868205@N00/21807915246)
- **Ipoh White Coffee** — Try at original kopitiams (Sin Yoon Loong, Nam Heong, Chang Jiang)
  [![Ipoh White Coffee](../static/img/ipoh-white-coffee.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=47634510)
- **Gerbang Malam Night Market** — Souvenirs, snacks, local market atmosphere
  [![Gerbang Malam Night Market](../static/img/gerbang-malam-night-market.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/200840969@N07/53789093850)

**Notes:** Quieter pace; mix of culture, nature, and family activities

### Segment 4: Melaka (2 nights)

- **Dates:** 11 December – 12 December 2026
- **Arrival:** 11 December
- **Transport from Ipoh:** Car or bus (~4–5 hours)
- **Accommodation:** 2 nights, [Wayfarer Guest House](https://www.booking.com/hotel/my/wayfarer-guest-house-jonker-street-melaka.en-gb.html)
- **Departure:** 13 December

**Popular Activities:**

- **Melaka River Cruise** — Boat ride featuring historic buildings, street art, and picturesque bridges (day and night options)
  [![Melaka River Cruise](../static/img/melaka-river-cruise.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/53663438@N00/2730880480)
- **Jonker Street (Jonker Walk Night Market)** — Bustling night market with street food, souvenirs, performances, street games
  [![Jonker Street (Jonker Walk Night Market)](../static/img/jonker-street-jonker-walk-night-market.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/17868205@N00/7438922182)
- **The Shore Oceanarium** — Modern aquarium with interactive exhibits, touch pools, 3D movies
  [![The Shore Oceanarium](../static/img/the-shore-oceanarium.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/77487402@N00/16222577487)
- **Menara Taming Sari (Revolving Tower)** — 80-meter rotating observation tower with panoramic views
  [![Menara Taming Sari (Revolving Tower)](../static/img/menara-taming-sari-revolving-tower.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/54945394@N00/2803019112)
- **A'Famosa Resort Complex** — A'Famosa Water Theme Park (slides, pools, lazy river), Safari Wonderland, Old West carnival
  [![A'Famosa Resort Complex](../static/img/a-famosa-resort-complex.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/27311527@N00/8598713)
- **Melaka Zoo & Night Safari** — Over 1,200 animals; night safari to spot nocturnal species
  [![Melaka Zoo & Night Safari](../static/img/melaka-zoo-night-safari.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=128244534)
- **Upside Down House Melaka** — Wacky museum with everything turned upside down (great for family photos)
  [![Upside Down House Melaka](../static/img/upside-down-house-melaka.jpg)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=75099369)
- **Malacca Butterfly & Reptile Sanctuary** — Colorful butterflies, reptiles, landscaped park
  [![Malacca Butterfly & Reptile Sanctuary](../static/img/malacca-butterfly-reptile-sanctuary.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/24319863@N00/16028783267)

**Notes:** UNESCO World Heritage Site; compact historic center; excellent food and night markets

### Segment 5: Kuala Lumpur (4 nights)

- **Dates:** 13 December – 16 December 2026
- **Arrival:** 13 December
- **Transport from Melaka:** Car (~2 hours) or bus (RM 20–40 per person, ~AUD 6–12)
- **Accommodation:** 4 nights
- **Departure flight:** 17 December

**Popular Activities:**

- **Petronas Twin Towers & KLCC Park** — Marvel at iconic towers, observation deck & sky bridge; KLCC Park has playground, water fountains, wading pool
  [![Petronas Twin Towers & KLCC Park](../static/img/petronas-twin-towers-klcc-park.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/7839604@N08/6843884439)
- **Aquaria KLCC** — World-class oceanarium with 90-meter underwater tunnel featuring sharks, rays, turtles
  [![Aquaria KLCC](../static/img/aquaria-klcc.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/35470197@N08/3933449022)
- **Zoo Negara (National Zoo)** — Over 5,000 animals including giant pandas; interactive wildlife experiences
  [![Zoo Negara (National Zoo)](../static/img/zoo-negara-national-zoo.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/61295396@N08/5665472571)
- **Sunway Lagoon Theme Park** — Massive park with water slides, amusement rides, wildlife park, surf beach
  [![Sunway Lagoon Theme Park](../static/img/sunway-lagoon-theme-park.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/8721758@N06/4551478313)
- **Kidzania Kuala Lumpur** — Interactive "mini city" where kids role-play 70+ professions
  [![Kidzania Kuala Lumpur](../static/img/kidzania-kuala-lumpur.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/62912989@N00/2736094522)
- **Batu Caves** — Colorful stairs, monkeys, impressive cave temples; popular with families
  [![Batu Caves](../static/img/batu-caves.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/96143003@N00/268338667)
- **Berjaya Times Square Theme Park** — Asia's largest indoor theme park
  [![Berjaya Times Square Theme Park](../static/img/berjaya-times-square-theme-park.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/54550615@N03/15669877924)
- **Central Market & Petaling Street (Chinatown)** — Cultural shopping, souvenirs, authentic street food
  [![Central Market & Petaling Street (Chinatown)](../static/img/central-market-petaling-street-chinatown.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/90267219@N00/4988930740)
- **KL Bird Park** — World's largest free-flight walk-in aviary with 3,000+ birds
  [![KL Bird Park](../static/img/kl-bird-park.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/8136604@N05/5698439329)
- **Thean Hou Temple** — Beautiful colorful Chinese temple on a hill with city views
  [![Thean Hou Temple](../static/img/thean-hou-temple.jpg)](https://www.flickr.com/photos/35470197@N08/3876394841)

**Notes:** Modern cosmopolitan city; excellent shopping malls with food courts; convenient Grab ride-hailing app

## Logistics

### Getting Around

- **Inter-city transport:** ETS train preferred where available (Penang→Ipoh) for comfort
- **Local transport:** Taxis, Grab app, tour operators
- **Rental car option:** Available if family prefers flexibility and luggage space

### Accommodation

- Book in advance (4–6 months prior)
- Mix of beachside resorts and city center hotels recommended
- Check family-friendly amenities (pools, kids activities)

### Budget Estimates (per day, family of 4)

- Accommodation: RM 300–600 (~AUD 90–180)
- Meals: RM 100–200 (~AUD 30–60)
- Transport (local): RM 50–150 (~AUD 15–45)
- Activities: RM 100–300 (~AUD 30–90)
